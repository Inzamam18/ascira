ASCIRA

"ASCIRA: A Beacon of Light Guiding You to a Brighter Future."

Vision

Our vision is to build a platform where every citizen has easy access to accurate and useful data. We aim to create an ecosystem that contributes to the nation's progress by sharing important information about unemployment, literacy, and other key metrics through a powerful API.

Mission

Our mission is to develop a robust API that provides simple and secure access to data related to unemployment and literacy. We are committed to fostering technology that makes data easily available for public use, thereby assisting in research, policy-making, and social reforms.

Goals

Our goals include building a comprehensive database that incorporates unemployment and literacy data using modern technology. We are focused on creating a system that provides this data to users instantly and effectively.

About Us

ASCIRA is an initiative by the Government of India aimed at making critical social and economic data transparent and accessible to all citizens. Our core objective is to democratize data by providing a powerful, easy-to-use API that serves as a central hub for information related to unemployment, literacy, and other key demographic indicators.

Through this platform, we strive to empower researchers, students, journalists, and policymakers with the data they need to drive informed decisions and create impactful solutions. By breaking down data silos, we are contributing to a more transparent, efficient, and data-driven governance model for a progressive India.

Important Statistics

Literacy Rate in Bihar

As per the latest available data, the literacy rate in Bihar is approximately 61.8%, which is the lowest among all Indian states. The government is actively implementing various schemes to improve this rate.

Employment Rate in Maharashtra

Maharashtra has one of the highest employment rates in the country, with an urban employment rate of around 94.5% and a rural rate of 96.1% as of recent reports. It is a major economic hub.

Population of India

India's population is estimated to be over 1.4 billion, making it the most populous country in the world. This demographic trend presents both challenges and opportunities for growth and development.

Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

A flagship scheme of the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) implemented by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC). The objective of this Scheme is to enable a large number of Indian youth to take up industry-relevant skill training that will help them in securing a better livelihood.

Ayushman Bharat Yojana

A national public health insurance fund of the Government of India that aims to provide free access to health insurance coverage for low-income earners in the country. It is a major step towards providing Universal Health Coverage.

Digital India Programme

A flagship programme of the Government of India with a vision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. The programme aims to ensure that government services are made available to citizens electronically.
